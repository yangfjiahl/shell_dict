<h2>语法</h2> 
<p>unset [-fV] [变量名或函数名]</p> 
<h2>功能介绍</h2> 
<p>unset 可删除变量或函数</p> 
<h2>参数</h2> 
<table> 
 <thead> 
  <tr> 
   <th align="left">参数</th> 
   <th align="left">作用</th> 
  </tr> 
 </thead> 
 <tbody> 
  <tr> 
   <td align="left">-f</td> 
   <td align="left">仅删除函数</td> 
  </tr> 
  <tr> 
   <td align="left">-v</td> 
   <td align="left">仅删除变量</td> 
  </tr> 
 </tbody> 
</table> 
<h2>例子</h2> 
<p>删除TOM变量</p> 
<pre><code class="language-bash"># unset TOM
</code></pre>
