<h2>语法</h2> 
<p>unalias [-a] [别名]</p> 
<h2>功能介绍</h2> 
<p>unalias 可删除别名设定。</p> 
<h2>参数</h2> 
<table> 
 <thead> 
  <tr> 
   <th align="left">参数</th> 
   <th align="left">作用</th> 
  </tr> 
 </thead> 
 <tbody> 
  <tr> 
   <td align="left">-a</td> 
   <td align="left">删除全部别名</td> 
  </tr> 
 </tbody> 
</table> 
<h2>例子</h2> 
<p>删除copy别名</p> 
<pre><code class="language-bash"># alias copy
</code></pre>
